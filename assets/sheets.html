Graph Types and Visualizations
Sheets

graph types and visualizations can be used to display different types of data,
including time series data, categorical data, geographic data, and hierarchical data. By selecting the
appropriate graph type or visualization, you can effectively communicate insights and
patterns in your data to your audience

Record Type of Countries

A population forecasting record type of countries graph can be used to visualize and analyze the expected
population growth or decline of different countries over time




Population Trends Over the Years

A Population Trends Over the Years graph is used to visualize and analyze changes in the population of a
particular region or country over a period of time




Population Trends Over the Years by Sex

A Population Trends Over the Years by Sex graph is used to visualize and analyze changes in the
population of a particular region or country over time, by gender




Cities with Highest Average Population

A Cities with Highest Average Population graph is used to visualize and analyze the average population
size of different cities, regions or countries




Countries by Highest Avg Population from
2000 - 2014

A Countries by Highest Average Population from 2000 - 2014 graph is used to visualize and analyze the
average population size of different countries over a specific time period




Population by City Type

A Population by City Type graph is used to visualize and analyze the population size of different
types of cities, such as urban, suburban, or rural areas




Population of Cities by Year

A Population of Cities by Year graph is used to visualize and analyze the population size of a
particular city over a period of time
